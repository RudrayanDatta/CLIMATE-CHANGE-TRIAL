> A curated list of impactful resources, tools, data, frameworks, and organizations dedicated to understanding and addressing climate change.

Climate change is one of the most pressing global issues of our time. This list highlights high-quality, openly accessible resources for research, policy, mitigation, adaptation, and education.

## Tools & Software

- **[Climate TRACE](https://www.climatetrace.org/)** – Tracks greenhouse gas emissions globally using satellite imagery and AI.
- **[En-ROADS](https://www.climateinteractive.org/tools/en-roads/)** – A global climate simulator for testing policy and technology solutions.
- **[Project Drawdown Tools](https://drawdown.org/solutions)** – Practical tools and resources focused on reducing carbon emissions.
- **[Open Climate](https://openclimate.network/)** – Open-source platform for global climate accounting and governance.
  
## Datasets & APIs

- **[NASA EarthData](https://earthdata.nasa.gov/)** – Open data from NASA missions related to Earth systems and climate.
- **[NOAA Climate Data Online](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/cdo-web/)** – Extensive weather, ocean, and climate datasets.
- **[Copernicus Climate Data Store](https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/)** – European climate data hub powered by ECMWF.
- **[Berkeley Earth](http://berkeleyearth.org/data/)** – Temperature and air pollution datasets from independent analysis.
- **[UNFCCC Data Interface](https://di.unfccc.int/)** – Emission inventories and national reports submitted to the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change.

## Research & Reports

- **[IPCC Reports](https://www.ipcc.ch/reports/)** – Authoritative scientific assessments on climate change from the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change.
- **[NASA Climate Science](https://climate.nasa.gov/)** – Overview of climate science missions, models, and findings.
- **[Our World in Data: CO₂ and Climate Change](https://ourworldindata.org/co2-and-other-greenhouse-gas-emissions)** – Data visualizations and research on emissions and energy.
- **[Global Carbon Atlas](http://www.globalcarbonatlas.org/)** – Research and data on carbon fluxes and emissions.

## Educational Resources

- **[ClimateScience](https://climate-science.org/)** – Youth-focused platform with explainers, courses, and graphics.
- **[MIT Climate Portal](https://climate.mit.edu/)** – Reliable answers to climate questions and explanations from MIT experts.
- **[Yale Climate Connections](https://yaleclimateconnections.org/)** – Daily reporting on climate change and its solutions.
- **[Khan Academy: Climate Change](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/biology/ecology/human-impact-on-ecosystems/v/climate-change)** – Free learning videos on climate topics.

## Mitigation & Adaptation Projects

- **[Project Drawdown](https://drawdown.org/)** – Comprehensive set of solutions for reducing global warming.
- **[Rewiring America](https://www.rewiringamerica.org/)** – Tools and policy resources for electrifying homes, businesses, and vehicles.
- **[Global Covenant of Mayors](https://www.globalcovenantofmayors.org/)** – Network of cities committed to climate action and resilience.

## Organizations & Initiatives

- **[UNFCCC](https://unfccc.int/)** – UN body for international climate negotiations and treaties.
- **[CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project)](https://www.cdp.net/)** – Global disclosure system for managing environmental impacts.
- **[350.org](https://350.org/)** – Grassroots movement for climate justice and clean energy.
- **[Climate Action Tracker](https://climateactiontracker.org/)** – Tracks climate policies and pledges by country.

## Developer Tools & Libraries

- **[climate-machine](https://github.com/CliMA/ClimateMachine.jl)** – A Julia-based tool for climate modeling.
- **[carbon-aware-sdk](https://github.com/Green-Software-Foundation/carbon-aware-sdk)** – SDK for building carbon-aware applications.

## Communities & Advocacy

- **[ClimateAction.tech](https://climateaction.tech/)** – A community of tech workers taking climate action.
- **[Work on Climate](https://www.workonclimate.org/)** – Community for professionals seeking climate-impactful work.
- **[Climatebase](https://climatebase.org/)** – Job board and talent network for climate-focused careers.
